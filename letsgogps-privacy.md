# Privacy Policy / 隱私政策                                                                                                                                                        
                                                            
**Last updated / 最後更新：2026-03-10**                                                                                                                                            
   
---                                                                                                                                                                                
                                                            
## English                                                                                                                                                                         
                                                            
### Overview

Let's Go GPS ("the App") is developed by moooo_works. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.                                                         
   
### Information We Do NOT Collect                                                                                                                                                  
                                                            
- We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.                                                                                                   
- We do not require account registration or login.        
- GPS location data you enter is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers.                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                     
### Permissions Used                                                                                                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                     
| Permission | Purpose |                                                                                                                                                           
|---|---|                                                 
| `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` / `ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION` | Required by Android to set a mock GPS location |                                                                             
| `INTERNET` | Required for Google Maps display and advertisements |                                                                                                               
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | Show a foreground service notification while location simulation is active |                                                                              
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | Keep the mock location service running in the background |                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                     
### Third-Party Services                                                                                                                                                           
                                                            
This App uses the following third-party SDKs that may collect data per their own policies:                                                                                         
                                                            
- **Google Maps SDK** — [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)                                                                                               
- **Google AdMob** — [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) / [Ad personalization](https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922)
                                                                                                                                                                                     
### Advertising                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                     
The free version of this App displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt out via your device's ad settings.       
   
### Children's Privacy                                                                                                                                                             
                                                            
This App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.                                                                                   
   
### Changes to This Policy                                                                                                                                                         
                                                            
We may update this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.                                                                                          
   
### Contact                                                                                                                                                                        
                                                            
For questions, contact: **your@email.com**

## 繁體中文                                                                                                                                                                        
   
### 概述                                                                                                                                                                           
                                                            
Let's Go GPS（以下簡稱「本應用程式」）由 moooo_works 開發。本政策說明您使用本應用程式時，我們如何處理相關資訊。                                                                    
                                                            
### 我們不收集的資料                                                                                                                                                               
                                                            
- 本應用程式不收集、儲存或傳輸任何個人識別資訊。                                                                                                                                   
- 不需要帳號註冊或登入。                                  
- 您輸入的 GPS 位置資料僅在您的裝置上處理，不會傳送至我們的伺服器。                                                                                                                
                                                                                                                                                                                     
### 使用的權限                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                     
| 權限 | 用途 |                                                                                                                                                                    
|---|---|                                                 
| `ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` / `ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION` | Android 設定模擬 GPS 位置的必要權限 |
| `INTERNET` | Google 地圖顯示及廣告投放 |                                                                                                                                         
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | 位置模擬執行中時顯示前景服務通知 |                                                                                                                        
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` | 在背景持續執行模擬位置服務 |                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                     
### 第三方服務                                            
                                                                                                                                                                                     
本應用程式使用以下第三方 SDK，這些服務可能依其各自政策收集資料：                                                                                                                   
   
- **Google Maps SDK** — [Google 隱私權政策](https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=zh-TW)                                                                                          
- **Google AdMob** — [Google 隱私權政策](https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=zh-TW) / [廣告個人化設定](https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922?hl=zh-TW)
                                                                                                                                                                                     
### 廣告                                                  
                                                                                                                                                                                     
免費版本會顯示由 Google AdMob 提供的廣告。AdMob 可能使用裝置識別碼提供個人化廣告。您可透過裝置廣告設定選擇退出。                                                                   
   
### 兒童隱私                                                                                                                                                                       
                                                            
本應用程式不針對 13 歲以下兒童，且不會故意收集兒童資料。                                                                                                                           
                                                            
### 政策變更                                                                                                                                                                       
                                                            
我們可能更新本政策。更新後繼續使用本應用程式即表示您接受新政策。
